Beans

Description

If you want to be tall as hell, eat a lot of beans. Rice, beans, and chicken stew. Naija boy budget starter pack

it takes forever to make but worth it in the end. Excellent source of proteins too.

ingredients

  • uncooked beans
  • water
  • salt to taste
  • lots of patience

steps

  1. wash beans and put in pot
  2. add water and salt
  3. put on stove and let it boil
  4. once the water is all gone, add more water and check to see if beans is soft enough
  5. repeat step 4 every time the water dries up or you can't deal with the crap anymore because why in the world does it take forever for beans to cook?
