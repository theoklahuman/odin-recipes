Chicken Stew

Description

This is what a Nigerian chicken stew looks like when it's finished. While it's one of the easiest things to make, it is very delicious, spicy and a good way to be introduced to NIgerian food.

You can eat this with rice, yam, spaghetti noodles, etc.

Ingredients

  • vegetable oil
  • garlic powder
  • tomatoes
  • onion
  • ginger powder
  • curry and thyme
  • chicken thighs (drumsticks)
  • seasoning powder
  • salt to taste

Steps

  1. put chicken in a pot and add 2 tbsp garlic powder, 1 tbsp ginger powder, chopped onions, 1 tsp rosemary powder, 1tbsp seasoning powder and about 3/4 tbsp salt to taste
  2. add 2 1/2 cups of water and let it boil for about 23 minutes
  3. when it's all cooked, remove the chicken and store somewhere. keep the chicken broth in a separate bowl to be used for the stew later
